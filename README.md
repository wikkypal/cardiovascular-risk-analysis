# ❤️ Cardiovascular Risk Analysis

## 📊 Overview

This project analyzes cardiovascular disease risk using patient-level clinical and demographic data. The goal is to identify key factors influencing heart disease and support early diagnosis.

---

## 🎯 Objective

To explore how variables such as age, gender, cholesterol levels, and exercise-related indicators impact the likelihood of heart disease.

---

## 🛠 Tools Used

* Microsoft Excel
* Pivot Tables
* Data Visualization

---

## 📂 Dataset

The dataset was obtained from a publicly available cardiovascular dataset used for analytical and research purposes.

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

* Highest heart disease prevalence occurs among ages **49–68**
* Male patients account for the majority of cases
* Exercise-induced angina is a strong predictor of heart disease
* Moderate cholesterol levels (200–299) show increased risk
